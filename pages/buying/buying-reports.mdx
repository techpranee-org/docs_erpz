# Buying Reports

Data related to purchases made by an organization can be analyzed and visualized through various reports available in the buying modules. Each report typically consists of three sections: a filter toolbar, a chart, and data. Changes in the filters and selections are immediately reflected in the chart.

## 1. Purchase Analytics

To access the report, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Key Reports > Purchase Analytics`

---

The **Purchase Analytics** report allows for the analysis of purchase-related data against suppliers, supplier groups, items, and item groups over a specified period and frequency.

## 2. Purchase Order Analysis

To access the report, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Key Reports > Purchase Order Analysis`

---

This report is useful for analyzing items and their billing status in a purchase order over a defined period.

## 3. Supplier-Wise Sales Analytics

To access the report, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Key Reports > Sales-Wise Sales Analytics`

---

This report provides extensive data on different items purchased from suppliers.

## 4. Items to Order and Receive

To access the report, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Key Reports > Items to Order and Receive`

---

This report visualizes and displays data for the purchase requests created.

## 5. Purchase Order Trends

To access the report, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Key Reports > Purchase Order Trends`

---

The **Purchase Order Trends** report allows users to visualize trends for purchase orders for a given financial year. Trends can be grouped based on suppliers, supplier groups, items, item groups, and projects.

## 6. Procurement Tracker

To access the report, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Key Reports > Procurement Tracker`

---

This report presents comprehensive purchase-related data, including material requests and purchase orders created for the items.

## 7. Other Reports

To access, navigate to:

`Home > Buying > Other Reports`

---

In the **Other Reports** section, the following reports can be accessed:

- Items To Be Requested
- Item-Wise Purchase History
- Purchase Receipt Trends
- Purchase Invoice Trends
- Subcontracted Item To Be Received
- Supplier Quotation
