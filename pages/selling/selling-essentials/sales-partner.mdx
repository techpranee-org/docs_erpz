# Sales Partner

**Sales Partners are people or companies that assist you in getting business.**

Sales Partners can be referred to by various names in ERPZ, including Channel Partner, Distributor, Dealer, Agent, Retailer, Implementation Partner, Reseller, etc.

For each Sales Partner, you can define a commission rate. When a Sales Partner is selected in transactions, their commission is calculated over the Net Total of Sales Order/Invoice or Delivery Note.

To access the Sales Partner list, go to:

- `Home > Selling > Sales Partner`

## 1. How to Create a Sales Partner

1. Go to the Sales Partner list and click on **New**.
2. Enter the **Sales Partner Name** and the **Commission Rate**.
3. You can also select the type of Sales Partner you're creating to identify whether they're a Reseller, Retailer, etc.
4. Click on **Save**.

![Sales Partner Example]()

## 2. Features

- **Commission Calculation**: The commission for each Sales Partner is automatically calculated based on the defined rate when they are involved in sales transactions.
- **Transaction Integration**: Sales Partners can be linked to Sales Orders, Delivery Notes, and Sales Invoices, making it easier to track sales activities and commissions.
