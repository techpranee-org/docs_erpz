# Country

In ERPZ, a **Country** list can be maintained, allowing you to assign appropriate countries to various entities and transactions as needed.

## 1. How to Add a Country

To add a country in ERPZ, follow these steps:

1. Go to the **Country** list and click on **Add Country**.
2. Enter the name of the country you wish to add.
3. Specify the **standard date format** used in the country (e.g., `DD-MM-YYYY`).
4. Specify the **standard time format** used in the country (e.g., 24-hour format).
5. List all applicable **time zones** for the country.
6. Mention the official **abbreviation** of the country (e.g., `US` for the United States).
7. Click **Save** to add the country to the list.

Once saved, the newly added country can be linked to various entities such as customers, suppliers, and transactions.
