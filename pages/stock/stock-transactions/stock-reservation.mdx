# Stock Reservation
Introduced in Version 15

Stock reservation, also known as inventory reservation, refers to the practice of setting aside a specific quantity of stock or inventory for a particular purpose or customer.

## 1. Prerequisites
- Enable Stock Reservation in **Stock Settings**.

## 2. Stock Reservation against Sales Order
1. Create a **Sales Order**.
2. Check the reserve stock for items you want to reserve.
3. Click on **Stock Reservation**, then select **Reserve**. 
4. Choose the warehouse and quantity, then click on the **Reserve Stock** button. 
5. Stock reservation entries are created against the sales order items.

## 3. Stock Reservation from Pick List
1. Create a **Sales Order**.
2. Create a **Pick List** for the Sales Order.
3. In the Pick List, click on **Stock Reservation**, then select **Reserve**. The Stock Reservation Entries will be created against the Pick List.

## 4. Auto Reserve Stock on Purchase
1. Navigate to **Stock Settings** and enable **Auto Reserve Stock for Sales Order on Purchase**.
2. Create a **Sales Order**.
3. Create a **Material Request** from the Sales Order.
4. Create a **Purchase Order** from the Material Request.
5. Complete the process by creating a **Purchase Receipt** for the Purchase Order. The stock will be automatically reserved upon submission of the Purchase Receipt.

## 5. Stock Unreservation
There are two ways to unreserve the stock:

### 5.1 Stock Unreservation from Sales Order or Pick List
1. Open a document and click on **Stock Reservation** > **Unreserve** button. The listed Stock Reservation Entries get cancelled.

### 5.2 Unreserve the stock from the Stock Reservation Entry DocType
1. Open a **Stock Reservation Entry** and cancel it by clicking the **Cancel** button.
2. Go to the **Stock Reservation Entry List**, select the entries you wish to cancel, and then click on **Actions** > **Cancel**.
